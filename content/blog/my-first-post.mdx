## Welcome

Hi! I'm a software engineer who loves building things. I'm coming back to the world of tech after a 10 month career break filled with travel, many conversations with friends old and new, and generally giving my brain a proper rest.

This is the first post on my new blog — a place where I'll share what I'm learning, projects I'm working on, and thoughts on software development, the state of tech in 2026, and beyond.

## What I Write About

Expect posts on:

- **Web development** — new trends and interesting technologies
- **Backend engineering** — APIs, databases, system design
- **Dev tools & workflow** — things that make me more productive and let me focus on *pure coding*
- **AI advancements** — everything I come across that could change the way we build software
- **Brain power** — how the brain works and how to get more out of it
- **Everything else** — occasional off-topic on whatever else catches my attention

## Why Blog?

Writing is one of the best ways to solidify understanding. If I can explain something clearly, I truly understand it. If I struggle to explain it, that's a signal to dig deeper.

It also gives me something to link to when answering the same question for the tenth time (most often to myself).
